F. Trung tm hi ngh
time limit per test
1 second
memory limit per test
256 megabytes
input
standard input
output
standard output

Chnh ph Siruseri xy dng mt trung tm hi ngh quc gia mi. Mt s cng ty n ng k thu t chc cc cuc hi ngh ca mnh.

Cc cng ty yu cu phi c s dng trung tm lin tc trong khong thi gian m mnh ng k. Trng phng tip th ca trung tm c quan im cho rng, cch tip th tt nht l m bo c cng nhiu cng ty c thu cng tt. R rng l c nhiu cch thc hin c iu ny.

V d, c 4 cng ty ng k thu, nh s t 1 n 4 theo trnh t ng k v danh sch thi hn s dng l nh sau:

R rng l ch c th p ng c yu cu ca 2 cng ty: 1 v 3 hoc 1 v 4 hoc 2 v 3. Yu cu ca cc cng ty 1 v 2 khng th ng thi p ng v b trng nhau ngy 9. L mt ngi chnh trc, trng phng tip th quyt nh a ra quy trnh chn nh sau: Lit k cc phng n p ng yu cu, mi phng n l danh sch cc cng ty c yu cu p ng c v chn danh sch di nht (cha nhiu cng ty nht). Trong mi danh sch cc cng ty c sp xp theo th t tng dn, tc l theo trnh t ng k. Nu c nhiu danh sch cng l di nht th chn danh sch c th t t in nh nht. v d trn, ta c cc phng n {(1, 3), (1, 4), (2, 3)} v v (1,3) < (1, 4) < (2, 3) nn hai cng ty 1 v 3 s c thu.

Yu cu: Cho nn s lng cng ty ng k thu, cc ngy u v cui trong thi hn thu ca mi cng ty (1n2000001n200000, ngy u v cui nguyn dng, khng vt qu 109109, ngy u nh hn hoc bng ngy cui). Hy xc nh mm - s cng ty c thu v a ra danh sch cc cng ty ny theo th t tng dn.

Input

Dng u tin cha s nguyn nn,

Dng th ii trong nn dng sau cha 2 s nguyn: ngy u v ngy cui theo yu cu ca cng ty th ii.

Output

Dng u tin a ra s nguyn mm,

Dng th 2 cha mm s nguyn theo th t tng dn danh sch cc cng ty c thu.
